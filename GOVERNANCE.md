# AAP Governance

AAP is an open protocol stewarded by Tokligence.

## Stewardship

- Tokligence maintains the canonical specification in this repository.
- Decisions are made through open RFC review and consensus.

## RFC Process

1. Open an issue describing the change.
2. Submit a PR updating the relevant RFC(s).
3. Review focuses on clarity, safety, and compatibility.

## Versioning

- RFCs are versioned documents.
- Backward-incompatible changes must be explicit and justified.
